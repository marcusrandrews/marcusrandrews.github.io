About Us & Plastic Welding

Performing plastic repairs requires plastic welding (wikipedia) which is an overlooked solution for damaged plastic items and it shouldn't be. It provides an economic alternative to replacement, disposal and re-fitting which saves time and reduces plastic waste.


Common items we can repair include, but not limited to (see more):

  • Poly Water Tank Repair
  • Poly Fuel Tank Repair
  • Boom Spray Tank Repair
  • Kayaks and Poly Boat Repair
  • Caravaning Products, Interior Fittings and Water Tanks
  • Wheelie Bin Repair
  • Poly Silo Repair
  • Feeding Troughs
  • Radiator Tank Repair
  • Auto Bumpers and Various Parts
  • Hard to Replace Items
  • Any damaged plastic items are worthy of getting in touch with us.

Plastic welding is unequivocally the best way to repair plastic items as silcone and glues do not work on some plastic types and different plastic need differernt sorts of glues to be effective. Plastic welding has the benefit of fusing plastic back together using heat creating a homogenous, strong and durable weld. It works very similar to metal welding. When performing a plastic weld it is important to prepare the surface of the damaged item properly beforehand and to use the correct welding filament.


At ACT Plastic Repairs we demand precision in the pre-welding surface preperation of damage items to deliver the most effective weld. We source high quality plastic welding filaments and tools from leading plastic welding suppliers such as Forsthoff and Techspan enabling us to work with all major plastic types including PP, HDPE, MDPE, LDPE, PVC, PC, PA, ABS, PET, PS and PMMA.
